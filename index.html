What is Patient Advocacy?

Patient advocacy means having a dedicated professional in your corner — someone who fights to make sure you get the care you deserve.

  • 🩺 Get appointments with the right doctors
  • 💊 Understand your medications and treatment plan
  • 📋 Navigate confusing insurance paperwork
  • 💰 Reduce or eliminate surprise medical bills
  • 📞 Communicate with hospitals and specialists

How Envion Health Works

Getting started with Envion Health is simple and completely free:

  • ✅ Step 1: Check your eligibility in under 2 minutes
  • ✅ Step 2: Get matched with your personal health advocate
  • ✅ Step 3: Your advocate reviews your insurance and needs
  • ✅ Step 4: We fight for your appointments, claims, and bills

Best of all — your Envion advocate is covered by your insurance.

Envion Health advocates are covered by your insurance plan — so expert support costs you nothing. You may only be responsible for a copay if your plan requires one, and if you have secondary insurance, it may cover that too. You get your Envion advocate for free.
